## Scored Questions — Forced Pairs (3 questions, 2 answers each)

### FP01 — Pair: A vs D (Grounded Mystic boundary) — NEW

> When you encounter something you can't explain — a coincidence that feels meaningful, a dream that lingers:

| ID | Answer | Scoring |
|----|--------|---------|
| FP01-A | You look for the pattern. There's a logic underneath, even if it's not obvious yet — and you want to find it. | A: +6, D: +2 |
| FP01-D | You follow it deeper. Whatever this is, it's pointing at something you haven't been willing to look at yet. | D: +6, A: +2 |

*Grounded Mystic boundary: tests logic-vs-depth orientation. Both options are active, engaged responses — neither is passive or negative. Someone who genuinely resonates with both is a Grounded Mystic candidate.*

**Desirability check:** Both options frame the person as thoughtful and engaged with mystery. Option A is the "investigator" and Option D is the "explorer." Neither is clearly superior — personal orientation drives the choice.

### FP02 — Pair: B vs C (Flow Artist boundary) — NEW

> You're offered two experiences at a retreat. Which draws you in?

| ID | Answer | Scoring |
|----|--------|---------|
| FP02-B | A movement practice with no choreography — just your body, music, and whatever wants to happen. | B: +6, C: +2 |
| FP02-C | A silent sit in nature — no agenda, no timer, just presence and whatever arises. | C: +6, B: +2 |

*Flow Artist boundary: tests body-vs-surrender orientation. Both are experiential, both have "no rules" framing. Someone who genuinely resonates with both is a Flow Artist candidate.*

**Desirability check:** Both are appealing wellness experiences. One is kinetic, the other is still. Neither implies more depth or sophistication. The retreat framing is on-brand without being exclusionary.

### FP03 — Pair: B vs D (flexible boundary) — NEW

> When something feels off but you can't name why:

| ID | Answer | Scoring |
|----|--------|---------|
| FP03-B | You tune into your body — tension, breath, gut feeling. It knows before your mind catches up. | B: +6, D: +2 |
| FP03-D | You sit with the discomfort and ask what it's trying to show you. There's always something underneath. | D: +6, B: +2 |

*Body-vs-depth boundary: both are non-rational ways of processing. Differentiates somatic intelligence from psychological depth-seeking. This pair was chosen over A-C because it discriminates between Embodied Intuitive and Shadow Dancer — two archetypes that share "non-analytical" orientation but differ in approach.*

**Desirability check:** Both options show self-awareness and emotional intelligence. One trusts the body, the other trusts the process of inquiry. Neither is clearly "better" — they reflect genuine orientation differences in how people process ambiguity.

**Note:** FP03's pairing (B-D) is adjustable. If Monte Carlo distribution testing in Phase 2 shows weak A-C discrimination instead, this pair can be swapped to A vs C without affecting the rest of the quiz.
