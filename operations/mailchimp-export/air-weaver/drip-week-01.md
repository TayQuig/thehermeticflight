** Why This Makes You Perfect for Tarot
------------------------------------------------------------

Here's what nobody tells beginners: Tarot isn't mystical randomness. It's a system with internal logic.
* 78 cards follow mathematical patterns: 4 suits × 14 cards + 22 Major Arcana
* Court cards form a matrix, not a mystery (we'll map this later)
* Spreads follow geometric logic, not arbitrary placement
* Suit progressions show narrative arcs with predictable structure

Once you see the architecture, everything else falls into place.

While other people struggle to memorize 78 individual card meanings, you'll see the system. You'll build frameworks that let you read cards you've never formally "learned." You'll understand the why, which makes the what automatic.

Your analytical mind—the one you've been told gets in the way—is actually your biggest advantage.
