### ***The Card System: 78-Card Structural Breakdown***

***22 MAJOR ARCANA: Archetypal Journey from Fool to World***

*Each card = one performer embodying an archetypal stage. Apparatus chosen to reflect symbolic meaning.*

*Examples:*
- 

*The Fool: Lyra, suspended above ground (the moment of new beginnings)*
- 

*The Hanged Man: Inverted ankle hang (voluntary sacrifice, shifted perspective)*
- 

*Death: Performer in motion through transformation sequence (unstoppable change)*
- 

*The World: Completion pose, grounded on a pole stage (integration, wholeness)*

*Progression visible through increasing choreographic complexity and mastery demonstration.*

### ***56 MINOR ARCANA: Four Suits, Complete Narrative Arcs***

***TORCHES / FIRE:** Wicks and flames climbing. Upward motion, reaching, ascending energy. Ace through Ten shows spark to full flame.*

***CUPS / WATER:** Lyra (aerial hoop). Flowing circles, emotional openness. Ace through Ten shows first feeling to emotional fulfillment.*

***SWORDS / AIR:** Aerial silks creating geometric patterns. Sharp angles, precise lines. Ace through Ten shows mental clarity to thought completion.*

***PENTACLES / EARTH:** The grounded energy of an iron pole, earthward connection. Strong forms, tangible presence. Ace through Ten shows material seed to legacy.*

***16 COURT CARDS: 4×4 Matrix Made Visible***

*Page through King progression shown in physical mastery levels.*

***Pages:** Exploratory positioning, learning relationship to apparatus
 **Knights:** Dynamic movement, pursuit energy, sometimes overextended
 **Queens:** Internal mastery, contained power, comfortable with apparatus
 **Kings:** Command presence, apparatus serves them rather than challenges them*

*Suit energy demonstrated through apparatus choice AND movement quality specific to each domain.*
