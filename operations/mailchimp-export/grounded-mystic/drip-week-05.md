## **The 16 Archetypes You Already Live
**

*|FNAME|*, court cards trip up almost everyone.

Sixteen personalities? How do you memorize that? When does the Queen of Cups show up versus the Knight?

But your *|TYPE_ADJ|* mind already has the answer. 

You don't need to memorize. 

You need to recognize.

The thing is… You already know them. 

You're not keeping track of strangers. You're meeting guides you'll recognize.

What if you already knew these people? What if they were characters you've encountered in every story you've ever loved? The curious student, the bold quester, the quiet sage, the commanding leader?

What if meeting them in the cards was simply meeting them again?

Reflecting on my Reiki journey, I can clearly see where I've embodied the Page, the Knight, the Queen, and finally the King.

I embodied the Page when I was pursuing my training. Every day I woke at 6am to study before my baby would need me. I would complete my meditation, studying, and application.

I embodied the Knight when I took action and began treating new clients for free to gain experience applying the information I had studied so dutifully.

I embodied the Knight again when I created my Reiki business and started The Reiki Goddess. I didn't wait until I was an expert. I answered the call.

As I grew through practice and experience, I became the Queen. I no longer doubted what I experienced during sessions. I understood when loved ones visited clients that they oftentimes would give me only one peculiar word to share. That single word always meant something profound.

Each and every time.

I embodied the King when I decided to finally teach Reiki Level One and Two; the outward expansion of my knowledge and experience.

You can find these characters in your own life as well. The mystical becomes practical when you recognize you've already lived it.

### **The Court Card Matrix Revealed**

It's not sixteen random personalities. It's Stage × Domain.

Four developmental stages (Page, Knight, Queen, King) × Four life territories (Torches, Cups, Swords, Pentacles) = Sixteen intersections.

                     (External)   (External)   (External)   (External)

Simple grid. Sixteen intersections. That's the whole system.

### **The Four Stages You Recognize**

PAGE = The Student/Apprentice Learning this domain for the first time. Curious, open, still discovering. Messages, new information, beginner's mind. The Page brings fresh perspective because they haven't learned what's "impossible" yet.

KNIGHT = The Active Pursuer Taking action in this domain. Bold, committed, sometimes reckless. Quest energy, pursuit mode, charging forward. The Knight has enthusiasm but not yet wisdom—they're learning through doing.

QUEEN = Internal Mastery Mature self-governance in this domain. Inner authority, emotional intelligence, contained power. The Queen has mastered this territory within herself—deep internal knowing that doesn't need external validation.

KING = External Mastery Established authority in this domain. Outward leadership, worldly recognition, command. The King has mastered this territory in the external world—able to lead others, recognized expertise.

Recognizing them gets easier when you think of them as developmental stages everyone moves through in every area of life.

### **How You Already Know These People**

**Page of Pentacles** = Your nephew learning to budget for the first time. Excited about his first paycheck, asking questions about savings accounts, studious approach to material responsibility.

**Queen of Cups** = Your friend who holds space for everyone's emotions without losing herself in them. She feels deeply, offers compassion freely, but maintains her own emotional center.

**King of Swords** = Your boss who makes clear, logical decisions even when they're difficult. He cuts through emotional noise to see what's actually true, leads through mental clarity.

**Knight of Torches **= Your colleague who launches three creative projects before finishing one. Enthusiasm and initiative everywhere, execution sometimes scattered, but that creative fire is undeniable.

You know these people. You've worked with them, lived with them, loved them. You've BEEN them in different domains at different times.

The court cards aren't mystical personalities to memorize—they're roles you already recognize from real life.

This matrix works whether you're just discovering tarot or have been reading for decades. The recognition is immediate—you know these people because you've lived alongside them.

### **Reading Court Cards in Practice**

The matrix works three ways:

As literal people: Someone in your life with these characteristics. When Queen of Cups appears in a relationship reading, it might be pointing to your friend Sarah.

As aspects of self: The part of you embodying this energy right now. When Page of Pentacles shows up, it's your apprentice-self learning something new about material reality.

As advice: "Embody this stage in this domain for this situation." Knight of Swords appearing? Time to pursue mental clarity actively, even if it means charging forward.

All three interpretations use the same simple matrix. Stage × Domain.

Both the spiritual archetype AND the practical role you inhabit.
